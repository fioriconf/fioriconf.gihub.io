Thorsten Lange

Thorsten Lange

Thorsten started his professional IT career in 1993. Over time, he held different positions like Data Organizer, Senior Consultant, IT Architecture Senior Specialist, and Product Owner.

Thorsten joined SAP in 2008 after having worked at SAP for 2 years as a contractor. He recently took over the position as Area Product Owner for all AI topics in UX Engineering. Until then he was a Product Owner for SAP Fiori elements floorplans for OData V4.

During his journey Thorsten worked with a lot of different technologies and programming languages like Lotus Notes, Adobe Flex, BSP, PCUI, Cobol, PL/1, Java, JavaScript, ABAP, ABAP Objects…
