FioriConf is in a very early planning stage

Schedule

  • Date: FioriConf is currently planned for Q1/Q2 2024
  • Will the talks be streamed? YES, FioriConf is an online conference
  • When will there be more information available? As soon as we have more information to share, it will be shared here

Speaking

  • Who can speak? Everyone that works daily with Fiori is invited to submit a talk
  • How to submit a talk? When the call for speakers opens, you can submit a talk
  • What do I need to present? Technically, you need a laptop, internet connection, microphone and camera. But most importantly: Fiori experience and the will to share your knowledge

Tickets

FioriConf will be streamed live, for free, on YouTube.

No ticket needed to assist the stream!
