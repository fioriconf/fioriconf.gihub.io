FioriConf is a free, live, online international conference for the Fiori community by the Fiori community. It's all about SAP Fiori.

FioriConf aims to be the conference for every technical Fiori user: developer, architect, admin. FioriConf bridges the gap between the technical conferences like UI5con and ABAPConf and the official SAP events like TechEd. FioriConf is about the poeple that bring Fiori to live at customers. How to get the Fiori app running, deployed, solve real world problems. It is for the people that bring Fiori to life.

You work with Fiori day in and day out? You put Fiori apps into productoin? You configure technical catalogs, pages and spaces? You define the landscape, governance and guidelines for Fiori apps? You write new, extend or customize Fiori Apps? Either if you are involved in the theory, the implementation or maintenance of Fiori at your employer or customer, FioriConf is made for you!

Conference Features

Community

From the Fiori community, for the Fiori community. Learn, share, connect with fellow Fiori developers, admin and architects. Plus: get in contact with the poeple that define SAP Fiori.

Open

Online, Free, Live. Everyone can join and participate. We try to lower the barriers as much as possible so that every person that works with Fiori can join.

Connect

FioriConf is not only to watch some recorded sessions. It is about interaction: live sessions, asking questions, get to know who is defining Fiori and how is used at customers.
